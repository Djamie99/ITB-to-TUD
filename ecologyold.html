The site of ITB is thriving with live

While exploring the site the horticulture students found loads of different species
which live on campus such as a foz, rabbits, hedgehogs, squirrels and a whole host of different
birds and insects, so it is safe to presume that there would have been even more so before the college was made.
Where the college is located is also called Buzzardstown due to the huge amount of Buzzard birds
that have lived there for hundreds of years.
